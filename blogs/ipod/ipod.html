Using an iPod as the primary music listening option

2024, Dec 9

There is a recent trend lately about using iPod - Apple infamous music device back in the 2000s, as alternative for most of music streaming services nowadays like Spotify or Apple Music. The medias claiming that using a external device for music playing gives you many multiple advantages that related to distraction in working and ownership of the music that you listening to.

So I bought one for myself to checkout if all the hypes were real. In October I bought a second-hand iPod Classic 5 (aka iPod Video) from Neyako for $50, which was a good deals at the momment since the demand for iPod was really high. The iPod has been replaced with a brand new battery, and modified with a brand new 120GB SDD.

After two months of using iPod (as the time this post is being writed), I can confirm that the hype was true. Using iPod does help me reducing distraction and procastination when comparing to listen to music on my smartphone, as I can more focus on most of my works. When using a phone, it can cause me to also have other unnecessary activities on my phone, whether it's browsing social media or anything else.
